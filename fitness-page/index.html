Smart Fitness Tips

  • Keep a water bottle nearby and hydrate often.
  • Start every session with a warm-up to avoid injuries
  • Try mixing cardio and strength workouts weekly.
  • Don't skip flexibility - stretch post-exercise.
  • Fuel your body with whole foods and protein.
  • Track progress—not perfection. Small steps lead to big wins!
